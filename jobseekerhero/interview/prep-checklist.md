# Interview Preparation Checklist

Use this checklist before every interview to ensure you're fully prepared.

## ðŸ“‹ Pre-Interview Checklist

### Research (Do this 1-2 days before)
- [ ] Research the company (mission, values, recent news)
- [ ] Research the team/department
- [ ] Review the job description thoroughly
- [ ] Check LinkedIn profiles of interviewers
- [ ] Research company culture and work environment
- [ ] Look up company reviews (Glassdoor, etc.)
- [ ] Check company's tech stack and projects

### Preparation (Day before)
- [ ] Review your CV and relevant project cases
- [ ] Prepare STAR method answers for common questions
- [ ] Prepare 5-7 questions to ask the interviewer
- [ ] Review your relevant skills and experiences
- [ ] Prepare examples that match job requirements
- [ ] Practice answers out loud
- [ ] Prepare your "tell me about yourself" pitch

### Technical Setup (For remote interviews)
- [ ] Test video conferencing software
- [ ] Check internet connection
- [ ] Test microphone and camera
- [ ] Prepare a quiet, professional background
- [ ] Ensure good lighting
- [ ] Close unnecessary applications
- [ ] Have backup device ready
- [ ] Test screen sharing if needed

### Materials to Have Ready
- [ ] Copy of your CV
- [ ] Copy of job description
- [ ] Notes about the company
- [ ] Questions to ask
- [ ] Pen and paper for notes
- [ ] Water nearby
- [ ] Portfolio/project links (if relevant)

### Day of Interview
- [ ] Get good sleep the night before
- [ ] Eat a good meal beforehand
- [ ] Arrive/join 5-10 minutes early
- [ ] Dress appropriately
- [ ] Turn off notifications
- [ ] Have a positive mindset

### During Interview
- [ ] Listen actively
- [ ] Take notes (if appropriate)
- [ ] Ask clarifying questions
- [ ] Show enthusiasm
- [ ] Be authentic
- [ ] Ask your prepared questions

### After Interview
- [ ] Send thank you email within 24 hours
- [ ] Document interview notes
- [ ] Follow up if no response after 1-2 weeks
- [ ] Reflect on what went well
- [ ] Note areas for improvement
