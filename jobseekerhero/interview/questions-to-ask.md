# Questions to Ask Interviewers

A curated list of thoughtful questions to ask during interviews.

## üéØ Why Ask Questions?

Asking questions shows:
- Genuine interest in the role
- Preparation and research
- Critical thinking
- That you're evaluating them too

## üìù Questions by Category

### About the Role
- What does a typical day/week look like in this role?
- What are the biggest challenges someone in this position would face?
- What does success look like in this role after 6 months? After a year?
- How is performance measured in this role?

### About the Team
- Can you tell me about the team I'd be working with?
- What is the team structure and how does collaboration work?
- How does the team handle technical disagreements?
- What is the onboarding process like?

### About the Company/Technology
- What technologies and tools does the team currently use?
- What is the company's approach to technical debt?
- How does the company support professional development?
- What are the company's biggest technical challenges right now?

### About Growth & Culture
- What opportunities are there for career growth?
- How does the company support learning and development?
- What is the company culture like?
- How does the company handle work-life balance?

### About the Process
- What are the next steps in the interview process?
- What is the timeline for making a decision?
- Is there anything about my background that concerns you?

## üí° Tips

- Prepare 5-7 questions before each interview
- Ask questions that show you've researched the company
- Listen to answers - they might lead to follow-up questions
- Don't ask questions that are easily answered on their website
- Save questions about salary/benefits for after an offer

---

*Customize these questions based on the role and company you're interviewing with*
