### Leadership & Initiative
- Tell me about a time when you took the lead on a project
- Describe a situation where you had to step up and take charge
- Tell me about a time you had to motivate a team

### Problem Solving
- Tell me about a time you solved a difficult problem
- Describe a situation where you had to think outside the box
- Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with limited information

### Conflict & Challenges
- Tell me about a time you had to deal with a difficult team member
- Describe a situation where you had to handle conflict
- Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned

### Communication
- Tell me about a time you had to explain something complex to a non-technical person
- Describe a situation where you had to give difficult feedback
- Tell me about a time you had to persuade someone

### Time Management & Prioritization
- Tell me about a time you had to manage multiple priorities
- Describe a situation where you had to meet a tight deadline
- Tell me about a time you had to say no to a request

### Learning & Growth
- Tell me about a time you had to learn something new quickly
- Describe a situation where you made a mistake and how you handled it
- Tell me about a time you received constructive criticism

### Teamwork & Collaboration
- Tell me about a time you worked effectively in a team
- Describe a situation where you had to collaborate with a difficult person
- Tell me about a time you helped a teammate

### Results & Impact
- Tell me about a time you exceeded expectations
- Describe a situation where you had to deliver bad news
- Tell me about your biggest professional achievement
