# Modo Seguro (Safe Mode) Feature - Itaú

## Context
Development of the "Modo Seguro" (Safe Mode) feature for the Itaú banking app, providing enhanced security for clients when they're away from home.

## Challenge/Problem
Clients needed better protection against fraud and financial losses when using banking services outside their home environment. The app needed a feature to prevent fraud and protect clients from potential financial losses.

## Your Role
Senior Android Developer - Responsible for maintenance, development, and evolution of the Itaú app, impacting more than 100 million clients.

## Actions Taken
- Developed the new "Modo Seguro" feature
- Implemented security measures for clients outside their home
- Created fraud prevention mechanisms

## Results
- **Prevented fraud and financial losses of up to 250 million (currency)**
- Provided much more security for clients outside their home
- Enhanced client trust and safety

- Android
- Android SDK
- Kotlin
- Java
- MVVM
- CI/CD
- Android Jetpack
