# Ligação Protegida (Protected Call) Feature - Itaú

## Context
Development of the "Ligação Protegida" (Protected Call) feature to protect clients from fake calls and phone-related scams.

## Challenge/Problem
Clients were vulnerable to phone scams and fake calls that could lead to financial fraud. There was a need to protect clients from these types of attacks.

## Your Role
Senior Android Developer - Responsible for maintenance, development, and evolution of the Itaú app, impacting more than 100 million clients.

## Actions Taken
- Developed the protected call feature
- Implemented call verification and protection mechanisms
- Created alerts and warnings for suspicious calls

## Results
- Protected clients from fake calls and phone-related scams
- Enhanced client security and trust
- Reduced vulnerability to phone-based fraud

## Technologies Used
- Android
- Android SDK
- Kotlin
- Java
- MVVM
- Android Jetpack
