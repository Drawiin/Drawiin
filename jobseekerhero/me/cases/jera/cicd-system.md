# CI/CD System Implementation - Jera

## Context
Establishment of a CI/CD system for a major client's application to reduce deployment time and ensure consistent releases on Google Play Store.

## Challenge/Problem
Deployment was manual and time-consuming. There was a need for consistent, automated releases to the Google Play Store with reduced deployment time.

## Your Role
Android Developer - Working on various client projects in health, energy, and transportation sectors, developing native Android solutions.

## Actions Taken
- Established a CI/CD system
- Automated deployment process
- Ensured consistent releases
- Integrated with Google Play Store

## Results
- **90% reduction in deployment time**
- **Consistent releases** on Google Play Store
- Automated deployment process
- Reduced manual errors
