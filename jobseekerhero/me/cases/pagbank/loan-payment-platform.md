# Loan Payment Platform - PagBank

## Context
Design of the Loan Payment Platform for internal payments, reducing costs related to bank slip issuance and improving financial efficiency.

## Challenge/Problem
The existing loan payment system relied heavily on bank slips, which had associated costs. There was a need to reduce these costs while improving payment efficiency.

## Your Role
Android Developer - Working within Invillia for PagBank, developing and improving the native digital bank app for Android, impacting more than 50 million clients.

## Actions Taken
- Designed the Loan Payment Platform for internal payments
- Reduced dependency on bank slips
- Improved payment processing efficiency
- Focused on cost reduction

## Results
- **Reduced costs related to bank slip issuance**
- **Improved financial efficiency**
- Faster payment processing
- Better user experience for loan payments

## Technologies Used
- Kotlin
- Android
- Java
- Payment processing APIs
