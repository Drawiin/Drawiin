# Check Special Renegotiation Feature - PagBank

## Context
Development of the Check Special Renegotiation functionality to facilitate debt renegotiation and increase client satisfaction.

## Challenge/Problem
Clients needed an easier way to renegotiate their check special debts. The existing process was complex and not user-friendly, impacting client satisfaction.

## Your Role
Android Developer - Working within Invillia for PagBank, developing and improving the native digital bank app for Android, impacting more than 50 million clients.

## Actions Taken
- Built the Check Special Renegotiation functionality
- Simplified the renegotiation process
- Improved user experience
- Focused on client satisfaction

## Results
- **Facilitated debt renegotiation** for clients
- **Increased client satisfaction**
- Simplified complex financial processes
- Improved user experience
