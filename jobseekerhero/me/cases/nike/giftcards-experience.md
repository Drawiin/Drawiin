# GiftCards Purchase Experience - Nike

## Context
Development of a new GiftCards purchase experience for the Nike Android app (both global and Chinese versions).

## Challenge/Problem
The existing GiftCards purchase flow had friction points that were impacting user experience and conversion rates.

## Your Role
Senior Android Engineer - Responsible for the development, maintenance, and evolution of the discovery journey (Purchase, Find Stores, GiftCards, and Product Details) of the Nike Android app.

## Actions Taken
- Created a new GiftCards purchase experience using Jetpack Compose
- Maintained interoperability with existing Views
- Focused on reducing friction in the purchase flow

## Results
- **50% less friction** in the GiftCards purchase experience
- Improved user experience with modern UI using Jetpack Compose
- Maintained compatibility with existing codebase

## Technologies Used
- Jetpack Compose
- Kotlin
- Android SDK
- Views (for interoperability)
