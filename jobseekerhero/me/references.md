## ðŸ’¡ Reference Guidelines

### Who to Ask
- **Former managers** - Best for leadership and work quality
- **Colleagues** - Good for teamwork and collaboration
- **Direct reports** - Great for leadership roles
- **Clients/Partners** - If applicable to your work
- **Mentors** - Can speak to your growth and potential

### What They Should Know About
- **Your role and responsibilities** - What you did
- **Key achievements** - Specific accomplishments
- **Skills and strengths** - What you're good at
- **Work style** - How you work
- **Character** - Your professionalism and integrity

### How to Ask
1. **Ask in advance** - Don't wait until you need them
2. **Be specific** - Tell them what role you're applying for
3. **Provide context** - Share the job description if helpful
4. **Make it easy** - Provide your updated CV
5. **Say thank you** - Always follow up with appreciation
