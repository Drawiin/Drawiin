## üéØ Rejection Stages

- **Application** - Rejected at application stage
- **Screening** - Rejected after phone screen
- **Interview** - Rejected after interview(s)
- **Final Round** - Rejected after final round
- **Offer** - Offer was rescinded or you declined

## üí° Common Reasons & How to Address

### "Not enough experience"
- **Action**: Emphasize relevant projects and achievements
- **Action**: Highlight transferable skills
- **Action**: Consider slightly lower-level roles to build experience

### "Not a good cultural fit"
- **Action**: Research company culture better before applying
- **Action**: Ask more culture-related questions in interviews
- **Action**: Be more authentic about your work style

### "We went with another candidate"
- **Action**: Request feedback (politely)
- **Action**: Continue building skills
- **Action**: Keep applying - it's often about timing

### "Overqualified"
- **Action**: Adjust your CV to match the role level
- **Action**: Explain why you're interested despite being overqualified
- **Action**: Consider if the role is actually right for you

## üìù Reflection Questions

After each rejection, ask yourself:
1. What could I have done differently?
2. What feedback did I receive (if any)?
3. What skills or experiences am I missing?
4. How can I improve for next time?
5. Was this the right role for me anyway?
