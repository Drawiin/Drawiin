# Standard Cover Letter Template

A general-purpose cover letter template.

---

**[Your Name]**  
[Your Address]  
[Your Email] | [Your Phone]  
[LinkedIn Profile URL]

[Date]

**[Hiring Manager Name]**  
[Company Name]  
[Company Address]

Dear [Hiring Manager Name],

**Opening Paragraph** - Hook and introduction
- State the position you're applying for
- Mention where you found the job posting (if applicable)
- Express genuine interest in the role/company

**Body Paragraph 1** - Relevant experience
- Highlight your most relevant experience
- Use specific examples from your work history
- Reference your project cases from [../me/cases/](../me/cases/) if applicable
- Connect your experience to the job requirements

**Body Paragraph 2** - Skills and achievements
- Discuss key skills that match the job description
- Include quantified achievements
- Show how you can contribute to their team

**Body Paragraph 3** - Why this company/role
- Show you've researched the company
- Explain why you're interested in this specific role
- Demonstrate cultural fit

**Closing Paragraph** - Call to action
- Reiterate your interest
- Request an interview
- Thank them for their consideration

Sincerely,  
[Your Name]
