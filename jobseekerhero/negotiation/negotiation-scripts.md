What to say in different negotiation scenarios.

### If the offer is lower than expected:

"Thank you so much for the offer! I'm really excited about this opportunity and the chance to work with [Company Name]. 

Based on my research of the market rate for this role in [location] and my experience with [specific achievement], I was hoping we could discuss a salary in the range of [your target]. 

I'm confident I can bring significant value to the team, particularly with my experience in [relevant skill/project]. Would it be possible to explore something in that range?"

### If you need time to consider:

"Thank you for the offer! I'm very interested in this opportunity. I'd like to take a day or two to carefully consider the details. Would it be possible to get back to you by [specific date]?"

## ðŸ’° Counter-Offer Response

### Making a counter-offer:

"I really appreciate you putting together this offer. I'm excited about the role and the team. 

After reviewing the offer and considering the market rate for this position, I was hoping we could discuss a base salary of [your number]. This is based on my research and my experience with [specific achievement/skill].

I'm also curious if there's any flexibility on [equity/signing bonus/vacation days/other benefit]?"

### If they can't meet your salary number:

"I understand the constraints. I'm still very interested in this opportunity. 

Would it be possible to discuss [alternative]:
- A signing bonus of [amount]
- An earlier performance review (at 6 months instead of 12)
- Additional equity
- Professional development budget
- Additional vacation days"
